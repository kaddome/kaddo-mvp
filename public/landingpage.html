Your "address" is safe with us!

Hoozad is a secure address repository which allows buyers to purchase gifts and have them delivered to your address without that address being disclosed to the buyer. Thus the purchase and delivery is secure, streamlined and most of all convenient.

We use SSL cryptographic protocols identical to that used by your high street bank to protect your information. We know that your private details and address are no less important than your money and we use the address provided solely for delivery of gifts and never share them with the sender or indeed anyone else!
